recipe for Kalkashi Soup

ingredients

  • powder kalkashi
  • water
  • salt
  • seasoning (maggi/rayco etc)
  • local seasoning ("kpagiri" aka "dadawa")
  • garden egg

steps

  1. get water in a pot equivqlent to the quantity of soup you want to meke
  2. add the two types of seasoning mentioned earlier, salt, thhe garden egg
  3. bring down the pot from your source of heat immediately it starts to boil
