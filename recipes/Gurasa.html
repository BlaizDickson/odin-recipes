GURASA

INGREDIENTS

  • flour
  • water
  • suger
  • yeast
  • salt

    • STEPS

    1. Add all the dry ingredients in a bowl and mix, addng a little cold water
    2. Make sure the dough is thick
    3. Press and stretch the dough and then cover it and place in a warm place for an hour
    4. Now heat a non-sticky pan
